FL Studio: Beginner Tips for Using Autotune Plugins

How to Use Autotune Plugins for Voice

  1. Record Your Vocal Track

    Open the Mixer, select an empty track, and record your voice.


  2. Add an Autotune Plugin

    Click on a slot in the Mixerâ€™s effect rack and choose an autotune plugin (e.g., Pitcher, NewTone, or third-party plugins like Antares Auto-Tune).


  3. Set the Key and Scale

    In the plugin window, set the key and scale to match your song for best results.


  4. Adjust Retune Speed

    Use the retune or correction speed knob to control how strong the autotune effect is. Faster speeds sound more robotic.


  5. Fine-Tune and Export

    Play back your track, tweak settings as needed, and export your finished vocal.


Tip: Experiment with different plugins and settings to find the sound you like!
